About Me

Oh, hello! I didn't see you come in. Well, don't just stand there, take off your jacket, have a seat, and let's get into it, shall we?

My name is Rich Coleman and I currently live in Chicago, Illinois, making a living as an Ad Ops Manager at Upshot Agency. If you're asking yourself,"What in God's name is an Ad Ops Manager?," don't worry! You're not alone. Putting it simply, I make sure that all of those advertisements you see on the internet are functioning properly for my specific clients. Yes, you're right, it IS a noble profession! Thanks for saying so.

When I'm not spening 40ish hours a week behind a computer in a aggressively air-conditioned office in River North, I'm spending 10 hours in a moderately comfortable classroom at Northwestern University, learning web development and computer programming in a part-time program. We experts call it a "coding bootcamp."

"Good heavens, Rich, a full-time job making sure doo-dads on the internet work AND you're learning how to code? Surely you have no other activities or committments?" Ohhhh, my friend, that's where you're wrong! I also spend a good chunk of time on stage doing Improv comedy at iO Theater, the home of longform improvisation.

"Oh... Improv, huh. Well, I, uh, better get going-" Nonsense, friend! You are under no obligation to come to any of my improv shows! Relax. However, if you would like to see me, you can see me perform with '66 Mustang, one of iO's Harold teams. It's hard to explain what a Harold team is to the non-improvisers, but just know that making a team is highly-coveted and me being on one absolutely makes me better than you.

I hope you'll take a look around my portfolio! If you're interested in working with me in any capacity -- I currently am taking on freelance web development, ad ops, and improv coaching work! -- then please contact me! Or just say hi!
