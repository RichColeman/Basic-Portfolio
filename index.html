About Me


Oh, hello! I didn't see you come in. Well, don't just stand there, take off your jacket, have a seat, and let's get into it, shall we?

My name is Rich Coleman and I currently live in Chicago, Illinois, making a living as an Ad Ops Manager at Upshot Agency. If you're asking yourself,"What in God's name is an Ad Ops Manager?," don't worry! You're not alone. Putting it simply, I make sure that all of those advertisements you see on the internet are functioning properly for my specific clients. Yes, you're right, it IS a noble profession! Thanks for saying so.

When I'm not spening 40ish hours a week behind a computer in a aggressively air-conditioned office in River North, I'm spending 10 hours in a moderately comfortable classroom at Northwestern University, learning web development and computer programming in a part-time program. We experts call it a "coding bootcamp."
